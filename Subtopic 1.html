Subtopic 1: Online Platforms


In this resource, it mentions all the different benefits like cloud computing and how it has helped shape the education system. By using cloud computing, this platform allows students and faculty to store documents into the cloud. By doing this, it helps keep all documents given more organized so that the important ones don't get lost. It also allows them to access these documents wherever and whenever. Services like these are crucial in the educational system because it helps students become more responsible for their work while keeping it organized at the same time. The same goes for teachers, they have many documents to store like lesson plans and grades, keeping those in organized folders in the cloud can help prevent them from getting lost.
Gamification uses gaming features to make learning more interactive. Students can complete interactive quizzes or content related games to help motivate them to study the curriculum.Websites like Kahoot make fun interactive quizzes to help students understand the material they’re being taught. The goal of gamification is to make learning more fun and more of a challenge for students!
AI can make learning more exclusive for students. AI can offer extra practice on sore subjects for students. Like Kahoot! And Quizlet, AI can provide study tools like quizzes and flashcards for different subjects.With the quick feedback AI provides, students can learn from mistakes they have made without having to wait on a teacher’s response.
Cloud computing allows students and faculty to store their work in an online platform. Platforms like Google Drive allow students to save their homework and class notes so they can be easily accessed at any time. Materials saved on platforms like these can be accessed at any time and on any device as long as it is saved on the cloud. This makes it easier for students to keep their work organized and all in one place. It can also allow students to share their resources with their classmates. Author:Infosys BPM
